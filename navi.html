Welcome To KGRL COLLEGE

K.G.R.L. College is located in Bhimavaram, a place hailed as ‘Second Bardoli’ by Mahatma Gandhi, a place where several thousands of Freedom fighters participated in the ‘Salt Satyagraha’ launched by Mahatma in the year 1930. The College enjoys a unique setting in the green fields. Its calm and serene atmosphere inspires the light of knowledge. It occupies a sprawling campus of 18 acres dotted with more than 25 buildings.

The Bhimavaram Education Society was established in July 1975 with a view to extend more education facilities to the people in and around Bhimavaram by establishing different Educational Institutions. As a response to the aspirations and enthusiasm of the people in general and the Bhimavaram Education Society in particular, Sri Kotikalapudi Govinda Rao came forward and offered munificent donation for the establishment of K.G.R.L. Degree College.
